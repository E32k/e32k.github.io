In this tutorial we will be creating an exo-car.<br>
This includes creating the model, UV-unwrapping it (texturing it) and jbeaming it.

I chose the sunburst since it was recently remastered and i hope this tutorial will last some time. It also has a nice engine with a low center of gravity, and with its suspension it can be fun both onroad and offroad.

# Making the directory

Navigate to your mod folder, you can do this by going in the mod manager, and clicking `Open Mod Folder`
